# Andrés's Daily Life Scenes

## Character Profile

**Name:** Andrés Gómez
**Age:** 42 years old
**Gender:** Male
**Ethnicity:** Colombian/Latino
**Profession:** Owner of a neighborhood tienda (small grocery/convenience store)
**Location:** Medellín, Colombia (Laureles neighborhood)
**Living Situation:** Apartment above his tienda with wife Lucía and son Mateo (12)

### Appearance
- **Hair:** Short dark brown hair, slightly receding hairline
- **Eyes:** Brown eyes, friendly expression
- **Skin:** Tan/olive complexion
- **Build:** Average, slightly stocky
- **Facial hair:** Neat mustache
- **Style:** Casual practical clothes - polo shirts, jeans, comfortable shoes

### Daily Context
- Opens tienda at 6:00 AM, closes around 9:00 PM
- Lives in apartment connected to store (common in Colombia)
- Known by everyone in the neighborhood
- Wife helps with store sometimes, son helps after school
- Takes siesta during slow afternoon hours
- Strong community ties, knows all regular customers by name

### Locations Featured
1. **Andrés's Bedroom** - Simple apartment bedroom above the tienda
2. **Andrés's Bathroom** - Practical family bathroom
3. **Andrés's Kitchen** - Small apartment kitchen
4. **Living/Dining Room** - Family gathering space
5. **The Tienda** - Heart of daily life, neighborhood store
6. **Tienda Storage Room** - Back room with inventory
7. **Street Outside Tienda** - Neighborhood life
8. **Wholesale Market** - Plaza Minorista for restocking
9. **Bank** - Weekly deposits
10. **Neighborhood Park** - Evening walks, son's soccer
11. **Local Bakery** - Getting fresh bread for the store
12. **Church** - Sunday mass
