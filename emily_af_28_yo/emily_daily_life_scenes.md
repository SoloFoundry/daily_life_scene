# Emily's Daily Life Scenes

## Character Profile

**Name:** Emily Chen
**Age:** 28 years old
**Gender:** Female
**Ethnicity:** Asian American (Chinese American)
**Profession:** Software Engineer at a tech startup
**Location:** San Francisco, California (Mission District apartment)
**Living Situation:** Two-bedroom apartment shared with roommate (Sarah)

### Appearance
- **Hair:** Long straight black hair, usually in ponytail or bun for work
- **Eyes:** Dark brown eyes, wears glasses (blue light blocking)
- **Skin:** Light tan/olive complexion
- **Build:** Slim, petite (5'4")
- **Style:** Casual tech worker aesthetic - jeans, sneakers, company hoodie or casual blouse

### Daily Context
- Works at a Series B startup in SoMa (South of Market)
- Commutes by bike or BART depending on weather
- Works hybrid schedule (3 days office, 2 days remote)
- Active lifestyle - goes to yoga, hiking on weekends
- Enjoys cooking Asian fusion meals
- Close relationship with parents who live in Sunnyvale

### Locations Featured
1. **Emily's Bedroom** - Modern apartment bedroom with IKEA furniture, plants, desk setup for remote work

4. **Living Room** - Shared space with roommate, TV, couch, plants
5. **Home Office Corner** - Desk with dual monitors, ergonomic chair
6. **BART Station** - 16th Street Mission station
