# A Day in the Life of Isabella: Visual Scene Descriptions

---

## DOCUMENT CONTEXT

### Project Overview

**Purpose:** This document contains detailed scene descriptions for generating educational anime-style images depicting a full day in the life of Isabella, a 9-year-old Colombian girl living in Cartagena with her parents and grandmother (abuela).

**Project Goal:** Create visual learning content that helps learners (children, ESL students, people with special learning needs, etc.) identify everyday objects and associate vocabulary with realistic images showing objects in their natural context.

**Why This Approach Works:**
- Child character highly relatable to young learners
- School vocabulary (classroom, playground, uniforms)
- Colombian coastal city context
- Multigenerational family dynamics
- Objects shown in real-world settings
- Cultural context embedded naturally

## Character Profile

- **Name:** Isabella
- **Age:** 9 (3rd grade, elementary school)
- **Gender:** Female
- **Ethnicity:** Colombian/Latina
- **Appearance:** Light brown skin (costeña coloring), dark brown curly hair usually in two braids or ponytails with colorful ribbons, big brown eyes, cheerful smile, small gap between front teeth, average height for age
- **Height:** Average for 9-year-old girl
- **Lives:** Two-story house in a middle-class neighborhood in Cartagena, Colombia, with parents and abuela (grandmother, 68)
- **School:** Colegio Santa María, private Catholic elementary school
- **Transportation:** School van (ruta escolar) or walking with family
- **Style:**
  - School: School uniform (navy blue jumper, white blouse, white socks, black shoes)
  - Home: T-shirts, shorts or leggings, sandals
  - Sleep: Nightgown or pajama set with cartoon characters
