# A Day in the Life of James: Visual Scene Descriptions

---

## DOCUMENT CONTEXT

### Project Overview

**Purpose:** This document contains detailed scene descriptions for generating educational anime-style images depicting a full day in the life of James, a 22-year-old African American culinary school student and line cook living in New Orleans, Louisiana.

**Project Goal:** Create visual learning content that helps learners (children, ESL students, people with special learning needs, etc.) identify everyday objects and associate vocabulary with realistic images showing objects in their natural context.

**Why This Approach Works:**
- Young adult character pursuing a culinary career — highly relatable to learners
- Culinary vocabulary (knife types, cooking techniques, kitchen equipment)
- New Orleans cultural context (Treme/Marigny, French Quarter, Creole cuisine, jazz)
- Daily routine from morning to late night covers wide vocabulary range
- Objects shown in real-world settings (apartment, school kitchen, restaurant)
- Cultural context embedded naturally through food, music, and architecture

## Character Profile

- **Name:** James
- **Age:** 22
- **Gender:** Male
- **Ethnicity:** African American
- **Appearance:** Dark brown skin, short black hair with close-cropped fade, clean-shaven, athletic build, medium height, bright smile with slight dimples, small scar on left hand from a kitchen knife accident
