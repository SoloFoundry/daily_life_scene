# A Day in the Life of Raj: Visual Scene Descriptions

---

## DOCUMENT CONTEXT

### Project Overview

**Purpose:** This document contains detailed scene descriptions for generating educational anime-style images depicting a full day in the life of Raj, a 72-year-old retired Indian American electrical engineer living in Edison, New Jersey, with his wife Sunita and dog Buddy.

**Project Goal:** Create visual learning content that helps learners (children, ESL students, people with special learning needs, etc.) identify everyday objects and associate vocabulary with realistic images showing objects in their natural context.

**Why This Approach Works:**
- Elderly character provides unique daily routine vocabulary (retirement, health, leisure)
- Indian American cultural context (puja, chai, Indian cuisine, cricket)
- Pet care vocabulary through interactions with dog Buddy
- Suburban New Jersey home and garden setting
- Objects shown in real-world settings
- Cultural context embedded naturally
- Multigenerational family connections (phone calls, weekend visits)

- **Name:** Raj
- **Age:** 72 (retired)
- **Gender:** Male
- **Ethnicity:** Indian American
- **Appearance:** Medium brown skin, thin white/silver hair neatly combed, trim gray mustache, thick-framed rectangular glasses, average height, slightly stooped posture, warm gentle smile, age spots on hands
