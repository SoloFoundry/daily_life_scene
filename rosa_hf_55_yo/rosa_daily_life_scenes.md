# Rosa's Daily Life Scenes

## Character Profile

**Name:** Rosa Mendoza
**Age:** 55 years old
**Gender:** Female
**Ethnicity:** Mexican American/Hispanic
**Profession:** Registered Nurse at Houston Methodist Hospital (25+ years experience)
**Location:** Houston, Texas (suburban home in Pasadena area)
**Living Situation:** Multigenerational home with husband Miguel, daughter Carmen (28), and granddaughter Sofía (4)

### Appearance
- **Hair:** Black hair with silver/gray streaks, usually in a practical bun or ponytail
- **Eyes:** Warm brown eyes
- **Skin:** Tan/olive complexion
- **Build:** Average, slightly curvy
- **Style:** Practical and neat, scrubs at work, modest casual at home

### Daily Context
- Works 12-hour shifts at the hospital (day shift: 7 AM - 7 PM, three days a week)
- Experienced nurse, respected by colleagues
- Helps care for granddaughter when daughter works
- Strong family and cultural traditions
- Active in church community
- Enjoys cooking traditional Mexican food for family

### Locations Featured
1. **Rosa's Bedroom** - Shared with husband, traditional decor with religious items
2. **Rosa's Bathroom** - Practical, shared bathroom
3. **Rosa's Kitchen** - Heart of the home, always cooking
4. **Living Room** - Family gathering space
5. **Dining Room** - Large table for family meals
