# Valentina's Daily Life Scenes

## Character Profile

**Name:** Valentina Restrepo
**Age:** 34 years old
**Gender:** Female
**Ethnicity:** Colombian/Latina
**Profession:** General Practitioner (Doctor) at a public hospital and private clinic
**Location:** Cali, Colombia (apartment in El Peñón neighborhood)
**Living Situation:** Lives alone in a modern apartment, long-distance relationship

### Appearance
- **Hair:** Long dark brown wavy hair, often pulled back for work
- **Eyes:** Brown eyes, warm expression
- **Skin:** Olive/tan complexion
- **Build:** Average height (5'6"), fit and healthy
- **Style:** Professional medical attire at work, elegant casual at home

### Daily Context
- Works split shift: mornings at public hospital, some afternoons at private clinic
- Commutes by car (silver Toyota Corolla)
- Health-conscious lifestyle - runs in the morning, eats well
- Close to family who lives nearby in Cali
- Passionate about public health and helping underserved communities
- Enjoys salsa dancing and live music on weekends

### Locations Featured
1. **Valentina's Bedroom** - Modern apartment bedroom, clean and organized
2. **Valentina's Bathroom** - Spacious, well-lit, elegant touches
3. **Valentina's Kitchen** - Open concept, modern Colombian home
4. **Living/Dining Area** - Comfortable apartment space
5. **Hospital Entrance** - Public hospital in Cali
6. **Hospital Hallway** - Busy public hospital corridor
7. **Consultation Room** - Where she sees patients
8. **Hospital Break Room** - Staff rest area
9. **Private Clinic** - More modern, smaller facility
10. **Car Interior** - Silver Toyota Corolla
